Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Luxanta collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

We may collect personal information that you provide to us directly, such as your name and email address when you make a purchase or contact us. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. We use cookies and technologies like the Facebook Pixel to enhance your experience and gather data about our website traffic and interaction. This helps us to offer better site experiences and tools in the future and to show you relevant ads.

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can also opt-out of marketing communications at any time. Please contact us at official.luxanta@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
